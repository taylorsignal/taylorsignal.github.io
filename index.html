Who is Taylor?

Taylor Trusty is co-founder and Chief Revenue Officer of Signal Insights, a better way of tracking your competitors used by over 200 companies, including Toyota Material Handling, Nasdaq, and Logitech. He's also co-host of Founder Secrets, a podcast interviewing inspiring founders.

Previously he co-founded Blackstone Media, a digital marketing agency acquired in 2018. A self-proclaimed computer nerd, Taylor taught himself to code at the age of 13 and started his first company at 19. He’s passionate about learning, sharing funny memes, and technology.

Born in Kentucky, Taylor now lives in New York City, where you can most likely find him catching a comedy show, biking around the city, or scouring the country for the best hikes.
